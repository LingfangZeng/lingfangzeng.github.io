About

Dr. Lingfang Zeng obtained his Ph.D. in Computer Architecture in 2006 from the Huazhong University of Science and Technology (under the supervision of Dan Feng). He was research fellow for 4 years in Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering, National University of Singapore, during 2007~2008 and 2010~2013. He publishes more than 50 papers in major journals including IEEE Transactions on Parallel and Distributed Systems (TPDS), ACM Transactions on Storage (ToS), Journal of Parallel and Distributed Computing (JPDC), IEEE Transactions on Magnetics, IEEE Transactions on Network and Service Management, and Software: Practice and Experience, and at international conferences, such as the USENIX Conference on File and Storage Technologies (FAST), the ACM/IEEE International Conference for High Performance Computing, Networking, Storage and Analysis (SC), the IEEE International Conference on Massive Storage Systems and Technology (MSST), the IEEE International Conference on Cluster Computing (Cluster), and the IEEE/ACM International Symposium on Cluster, Cloud and Grid Computing (CCGrid), etc. He serves for multiple international journals and conferences. He has authored and co-authored one book and two book chapters. He was on the Storage Challenge Finalist at ACM/IEEE SC06. He is a member of IEEE, ACM, and CCF.
