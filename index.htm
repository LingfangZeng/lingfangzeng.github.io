About

Prof. Dr. Lingfang Zeng is currently with Zhejiang Lab as a full professor and with the College of Computer Science and Technology, Zhejiang University, as an adjunct professor. He is the head of ZJ Lab-Enflame Joint Innovation Research Center, whose main goal is to R&D advanced AI chips and build large energy-saving intelligent computers with cutting-edge technology. He received his B.S. from Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST), Wuhan, China in 2000, M.S. from China University of Geoscience, China in 2003 and PhD in computer architecture, from HUST in 2006. Then he joined HUST in 2006 as Assistant Professor and then promoted to Associate Professor in 2009. He was a Visiting Professor for 2 years in the Zentrum für Datenverarbeitung, Johannes Gutenberg University Mainz, Germany, during 2016~2018. He was Postdoc Research Fellow for 4 years in Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering, National University of Singapore, during 2007~2008 and 2010~2013. His research interests include AI chip, emerging non-volatile memories and storage systems, big data and computational intelligence, and privacy-enhanced information storage. He published more than 100 papers in major journals, including IEEE Transactions on Parallel and Distributed Systems, ACM Transactions on Storage, IEEE Transactions on Computers, IEEE Transactions on Knowledge and Data Engineering, IEEE Transactions on Dependable and Secure Computing, Transactions on Information Forensics & Security, IEEE Transactions on Magnetics, Journal of Parallel and Distributed Computing, Journal of Systems Architecture, IEEE Transactions on Services Computing, IEEE Transactions on Network and Service Management, Information Sciences and at international conferences, such as FAST, SC, SIGMOD, MSST, IPDPS, CLUSTER, CCGrid, ICCD, etc. He has authored and co-authored one book and two book chapters. He has participated in the development of 11 industry standards and been granted 50 patents. Prof. Zeng is a member of CCF Board. He is the CCF Distinguished Speaker, the CCF Distinguished Member, and the member of IEEE and ACM. He was on the Storage Challenge Finalist at ACM/IEEE SC06. Dr. Zeng is currently serving in the Editorial Board of Journal of High-Performance Storage (JHPS) and was in the Review Board of IEEE Transactions on Parallel and Distributed Systems (TPDS) during 2020-2021.
