Research & Teaching

He teaches and does research spanning (1) high performance storage system, (2) big data and computational intelligence, (3) privacy-enhanced information storage. He is interested in making systems more high performance and high energy-efficient, trustworthy and transparent. His past research has spanned RAID, workflow, data privacy. He is always looking for strong students to join the group.
